Intro

SJSU Bike rental is a one stop solution for renting a bike you love. The application provides a bike of your choice for high performance ride in the city. We guarantee reservation in the time and place as per the customer needs, and provide instant confirmation on the reservation. We provide a wide range of bikes suiting all riding styles and terrain. We as a team would be working towards building a safe and secure solution for online bike renting. The application would cater to the requirements of both the customer and the admin.
